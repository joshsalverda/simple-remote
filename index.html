Tap to select things and to pause/play media.
Press for half a second to bring up the context menu and to stop when playing media.
Swipe up/down/left/right to navigate. When playing media swipe left/right to skip back/forward, respectively.
Pan up/down and hold to quickly scroll through long lists. Pan left/right and hold to speed through media.
Pinch out to go back in the GUI. I know this is awkward, it may change.

When input is requested the device keyboard should show up automatically, except on iPhone.

iPhone users will need to tap on the screen once to have the keyboard display. This is due to a technical limitation.
